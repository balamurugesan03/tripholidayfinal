Date Change Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Package Date Change Charges

We understand that plans can change. Our flexible date change policy allows you to modify your travel dates subject to the following charges:

Days Before Departure Date Change Charges
More than 45 days 5% of package cost + vendor charges
30 to 45 days 10% of package cost + vendor charges
15 to 30 days 25% of package cost + vendor charges
7 to 15 days 40% of package cost + vendor charges
Less than 7 days Date change not permitted
Note: Vendor charges include hotel, flight, transportation, and activity rebooking fees which vary by service provider

Flight Date Change

  • Flight date changes are subject to airline availability and fare difference
  • Airline rescheduling charges apply (typically $50-$150 per person)
  • If new fare is higher, difference must be paid
  • If new fare is lower, difference is non-refundable
  • Low-cost carriers often don't allow date changes
  • Our service charge of $25 per ticket applies

Hotel Date Change

  • Hotel date modifications are subject to hotel availability
  • Rate difference (if applicable) must be paid
  • Peak season bookings may not be changeable
  • Advance notice of at least 7 days required
  • Some hotels charge modification fees

Terms & Conditions for Date Changes

  • Date change requests must be made in writing via email
  • Maximum 1 date change allowed per booking
  • New travel dates must be within 6 months from original date
  • Date change cannot be combined with package modifications
  • Seasonal packages cannot be changed to different seasons
  • Group booking date changes require all members' consent
  • Special offer packages may not be eligible for date changes

Date Change Process

  1. Request Submission: Send email with booking reference and desired new dates
  2. Availability Check: Our team checks availability for new dates (24-48 hours)
  3. Quotation: We provide revised quotation with charges and fare differences
  4. Payment: Pay the date change charges and fare difference (if any)
  5. Confirmation: Receive updated booking confirmation with new dates

Name Change Policy

In addition to date changes, we also allow name changes under certain conditions:

  • Minor corrections (spelling errors) allowed free of cost within 24 hours of booking
  • Complete name change treated as cancellation + new booking
  • Flight tickets: Name changes generally not permitted (airline policy)
  • Hotel bookings: Name changes possible with hotel approval
  • Service charge of $30 applies for permitted name changes

Peak Season Restrictions

During peak travel seasons (December-January, Summer vacations, Long weekends, Festival periods), date changes may not be possible due to limited availability. Cancellation policy will apply in such cases.
