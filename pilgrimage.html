Before You Travel

  • Check temple timings and special days

  • Carry valid ID proofs
  • Book in advance during festival seasons
  • Check weather conditions for mountain temples

During Your Visit

  • Follow queue discipline during darshan
  • Avoid leather items in certain temples
  • Keep sacred places clean
